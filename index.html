Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Alpan Translate (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or account details.

The application works without user registration.

When you use the translation feature:

  • The text you enter is sent to external servers operated by Hugging Face for translation processing
  • The text is processed temporarily and is not stored by the application

2. How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Provide translation functionality
  • Ensure proper operation of the application
  • Improve performance and stability

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide translation functionality, Alpan Translate uses third-party services provided by Hugging Face.

The text you submit for translation is transmitted to Hugging Face servers solely for the purpose of generating a translation.

We do not store, log, or associate translation text with individual users.

Data processed by Hugging Face is handled in accordance with their own privacy policy.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Translation text is not stored by Alpan Translate
  • Text is transmitted to Hugging Face only for real-time translation
  • No personal data is retained on our servers

5. Cookies and Tracking

The application does not use cookies.

If analytics or crash reporting tools are used, they collect only anonymous technical data and do not identify individual users.

6. Push Notifications

If push notifications are enabled, they are used only for app-related information. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data during transmission, including secure network connections. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request information about data processing or request deletion of data.

Since we do not store personal data, most requests can be fulfilled immediately.

9. Children’s Privacy

Alpan Translate is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be available within the app or on the app store page.
